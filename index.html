Welcome to Merge Villa – where merge puzzles, interior design, and an engaging storyline come together! Help Alice restore her family mansion by solving puzzles, completing tasks, and uncovering secrets from the past.

Game Description

Merge Villa is a unique game that combines merge puzzles, home renovation, and thrilling storylines. Complete tasks, find rare items, and unlock new rooms!

  • Merge items – expand your game experience
  • Renovate the mansion – choose your interior style
  • Meet characters – uncover their secrets
How to Play :

  • Merge items of the same type to create new ones.

  • Renovate the mansion by choosing a style for each room.

  • Complete quests to unlock new features.

  • Uncover secrets and follow the storyline.

  • Use boosters and power-ups to speed up your progress.

Feature Keys

  • Engaging storyline – every room unlocks a new chapter.

  • House customization – choose furniture, colors, and styles.

  • Daily events – earn bonuses and rewards.

  • Interesting characters – each with a unique story.

  • Play at your own pace – no strict time limits.

FAQ
  • How can I get more energy?
    Energy replenishes automatically, but you can get extra by watching ads or using special boosters.
  • Can I change the furniture after selecting it?
    Yes! You can change the room design and replace furniture anytime.
  • Do I need an internet connection to play?
    Merge Villa can be played offline, but you’ll need the internet for updates and events.
  • Is there an age restriction?
    The game is suitable for all ages but is recommended for players 3+.
  • How can I get rare items?
    Some items can be obtained through merging, completing levels, or special tasks.
  • Will there be updates and new rooms?
    Yes! We regularly add new rooms, events, and stories to keep the game exciting.
  • How do I earn more coins?
    Complete tasks, finish levels, and participate in daily events to earn more coins.
  • Is the game free?
    Yes, Merge Villa is free to play, but there are optional in-game purchases for bonuses and special items.
